Ben Kirkbride

Director – Australian Institute of Health & Safety

I am honoured to have been elected as a Director of the Australian Institute of Health & Safety. Thank you to all members who took the time to vote and placed their trust in me.

I look forward to contributing to the Institute’s governance and supporting the ongoing growth, capability and connection of our profession.

My focus will remain on strengthening member value, elevating professional standards, and supporting the continued evolution of health and safety practice across Australia.

I will give this role the commitment, energy and integrity it deserves, working collaboratively with the Board, members, volunteers and the wider H&S community.
